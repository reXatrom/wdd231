Rexatrom Critic's Corner

Rexatrom Critic's Corner

This name captures the essence of the site a place for everyday users to be critics and share their opinions on books and movies. It's catchy, easy to remember, and clearly tells users what to expect.

Optional domain availability: Rex_criticscorner.ng or rex-critics-corner.net

Site Purpose

The site allows users to browse, rate, and review books and movies. It provides access to summaries, user ratings, and community opinions. The purpose is to help visitors discover great content through reviews and trending recommendations.

Scenarios

  • What are the top-rated sci-fi books this year?
  • Can I leave a review for a movie I just watched?

Color Schema

  • Crimson Red (#b31217) – Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • White Smoke (#fdfdfd) – Used as the primary background color for a clean look.
  • Charcoal Gray (#2c2c2c) – Used for body text and readability.

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for titles and review headers to add a classy feel.
  • Roboto – Used for all body text and UI elements for readability and clarity.
